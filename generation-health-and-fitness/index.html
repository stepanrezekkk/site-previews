Meet Your Coach

Built For The Community

Charlie Nield founded Generation Health & Fitness in 2022 with a clear mission: build an independent gym in Glasgow's East End where everyone feels welcome. Based at 96 David Street in Bridgeton, the facility combines a fully equipped gym with expert personal training and genuine community spirit.

Specialising in weight loss, muscle building, and general fitness, Charlie offers 1-to-1, group, and online coaching — each backed by customised training plans, full nutrition support, recipe books, meal planning, weekly check-ins, and WhatsApp access between sessions.
