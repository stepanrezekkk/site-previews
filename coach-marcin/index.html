Coaching That Sticks

Marcin Szarek has spent over eight years getting people in shape, helping them overcome injuries, building their strength, and making them more comfortable in their own skin -- all while keeping workouts fun and friendly.

Based at PureGym Fort Kinnaird in Edinburgh, Marcin works with everyone from complete beginners learning gym basics to advanced lifters preparing for physique and powerlifting competitions. His clients stay for years because the results never stop coming.
