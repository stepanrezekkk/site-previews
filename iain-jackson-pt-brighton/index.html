Meet Your Coach

Mindful Nutritional Physical

Iain Jackson has been a competitive athlete since the age of six, reaching county level in rugby, football, basketball and cricket before becoming a national champion in Tang Soo Do at 14. He spent eight years as a professional boxer with 22 years of total boxing experience, and has been a qualified personal trainer for over a decade.

His MNP training philosophy — Mindful, Nutritional, Physical — is built on the belief that optimum health requires all three components working together. Iain is fascinated by how bodies work and believes that effective exercise can stop every niggle and pain. He calls it building a pension for your health.
