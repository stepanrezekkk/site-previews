Primal Gym in Leith takes exercise back to its natural roots. Their mission is simple: get everyone moving through functional fitness, gymnastics, calisthenics, and mobility work that translates directly into everyday life.

With a team of seven specialist coaches — including backgrounds in martial arts, competitive gymnastics, sport science, yoga, and Spartan racing — Primal offers a depth of expertise that is rare in any single facility. No goal is too small, and absolute beginners are genuinely welcome.
