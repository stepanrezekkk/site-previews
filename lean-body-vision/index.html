Meet Your Coach

Results With Passion

Hannah Johnson has worked in the health and fitness industry for over twenty years. As founder of Lean Body Vision, she's passionate about results and about making exercise relevant and enjoyable — not a chore.

A Level 3 Personal Trainer, Level 4 Sports Massage Therapist, and qualified Nutritionist, Hannah also lectures at Coventry University and teaches fitness industry qualifications. In 2014, she founded The Juice House to fill the gaps her clients couldn't find with nutrition, snacks, and socialising.
